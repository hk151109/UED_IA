Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our library services and resources.

search

Our library is open Monday through Friday from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, and Saturday from 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM. We're closed on Sundays and public holidays. 24/7 access to our digital resources is available through the online portal.

Students and faculty automatically receive library privileges with their Somaiya ID. External users can apply for a library card by visiting the circulation desk with valid ID and address proof. Some restrictions may apply to external borrowing.

Students can borrow up to 5 books for 2 weeks, faculty can borrow up to 10 books for 4 weeks. DVDs and multimedia materials are available for 1 week. Reference materials and journals cannot be borrowed but can be used within the library.

Use our online catalogue to search by title, author, subject, or keyword. You can also use advanced search filters to narrow your results. For research assistance, contact our reference librarians who can help you find specialized resources.

Online databases are accessible through our website when connected to the campus network. Off-campus access requires login with your student/faculty credentials. Contact the IT help desk if you experience access issues.

You can renew books online through your account, by phone, or at the circulation desk. Renewals are subject to availability and no holds by other users. Books can typically be renewed once for the same loan period.

Late fees apply for overdue items: ₹2 per day for books, ₹10 per day for DVDs. Your borrowing privileges may be suspended if items are significantly overdue. Contact us if you're having trouble returning items on time.

OPAC does not allow online circulations by the members themselves at the moment. You have to obtain your books and other resources from the library itself.

Reservations can be made online on resources that are not currently available in the library you are looking into. To do that:

  1. Click on My Account link available in the OPAC Home page
  2. If you are logging in first time, system will prompt you to change the password
  3. Use your Member ID as Username and password to login
  4. Select your library name from the list and complete the Change Password transaction
  5. My Account Home page will appear - search the required book
  6. If a required book is not currently available in the library, Reserve option will get enabled
  7. Click Reserve link to reserve the book/title. System will generate Reservation number
  8. As soon as reserved title gets returned, you will be intimated through an automatic email

Or get in touch with librarian for assistance.
