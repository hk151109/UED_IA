What am I searching?

When you use the search bar above, you are searching across the Somaiya Library's entire collection. This includes:

  • Books: Physical books available in our libraries and our extensive collection of e-books.
  • Journals and Articles: Scholarly articles from a wide range of academic journals.
  • Theses and Dissertations: Research work from our students and faculty.
  • Audio/Video: Multimedia resources including documentaries and lectures.

This "Discovery Service" helps you find relevant resources quickly, no matter the format.
